* 年少时，
    
* 为喜欢、爱对的人，
    
* 倾其所有；
    
* 等他（她）老了，
    
* 诀别的时候，
    
* 留下悲伤的眼泪。
    

---

* When young,
    
* Gave everything,
    
* For the person liked and loved;
    
* Parted,
    
* Left sad tears,
    
* When he (she) was old.
