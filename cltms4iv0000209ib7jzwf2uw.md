* 我能够企及的只有，
    
* 眼前的那几颗“凡星”;
    
* 是渴望跃动着的，
    
* 灯、火。
    
* 那遥远模糊视线的东西，
    
* 根本没有必要望见。
    

---

* I can reach only few,
    
* “Ordinary stars” in front of me;
    
* It is the lamp, the fire,
    
* Longs to dance.
    
* Thing that far-off blurred vision,
    
* There is no need to see it at all.
