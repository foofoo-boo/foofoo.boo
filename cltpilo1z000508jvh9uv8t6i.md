* 微凉的风，
    
* 是夜对你的温柔；
    
* 梦境里的闲言碎语，
    
* 是现实于我的旁敲侧引。
    

---

* Cool wind,
    
* Is the tenderness of night to you;
    
* The gossip in dream,
    
* Is insinuation of reality to me.
